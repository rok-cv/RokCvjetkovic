ABOUT ME

I love exploring and learning about new fields

Ever since I was little, I have always researched and been interested in new places, attractions and extreme sports. The outside world fascinated me every day again and charmed me with a new place, plant, animal or tree canopy that I was able to climb. I have always dreamt of capturing the moment and save it. A few years passed and I got my first camera, it was a NIKON D3400. Photography impressed me and the ability to save the moment at the exact angle I imagined took over me. At the same time, I got to know the world of information technology and became acquainted with the web. The fact that I could realize my visions in the form of websites made me even more excited, paired with a healthy lifestyle and sports has only further strengthened new ideas and creativity, which develops into ultimate projects and life goals.

I have just finished high school for computer technician with excellent success and I am going to continue my journey at the faculty where I will get a deeper understanding about web and information technologies.
